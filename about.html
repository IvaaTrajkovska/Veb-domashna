Who I Am

Hello, I'm Iva Trajkovska. I have a deep passion for travel and exploration. Ever since I was young, I've been fascinated by different cultures, landscapes, and the adventure that comes with stepping foot in new places.

My Interests

My travel interests are diverse and ever-expanding. I find joy in discovering the unique charms of every place I visit. There is a quote that I really like, it says: "The world is a book and those who do not travel read only one page."

Why I Love Traveling

Traveling opens up a world of possibilities and broadens my horizons. It allows me to break free from routine, challenge myself, and gain a deeper understanding of the world we live in. Through travel, I believe we can foster empathy, respect, and appreciation for the diversity that exists across our planet. It's a continuous journey of self-discovery and personal growth that I cherish.
